## Regulatory & Market Context
- **California DROP enforcement: T-16 days (August 1, 2026)** — data brokers must retrieve DELETE requests every 45 days and complete removals within 90 days or face $200/consumer/day fines; 215,000+ consumers have registered; non-compliance penalties go live in 16 days
- **Connecticut privacy law signed May 27, 2026** (Public Act No. 26-64): requires data broker registration, creates new consumer rights, and mandates a centralized deletion mechanism by July 1, 2028 — first state to follow California's centralized-deletion model; creates a new addressable compliance market for removal services
- Data broker removal industry expected to reach 26 million users in 2026 per analyst estimates; category awareness accelerating ahead of CA DROP deadline
