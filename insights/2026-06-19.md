## Regulatory & Policy
- **California DROP (August 1, 2026 deadline — 43 days out):** ~543 registered data brokers must begin processing single-click deletion requests. CalPrivacy has not issued new enforcement guidance since January 2026; the August 1 hard deadline is the next inflection point. Paid consumer services have yet to publish any defensive messaging acknowledging the free state tool.
- **CalPrivacy Strike Force — ongoing enforcement:** Datamasters (Rickenbacher Data LLC) fined $45,000 for failure to register as a data broker under the Delete Act; enforcement sweep continues and is expected to expand ahead of the August 1 DROP processing deadline.

- **US House SECURE Data Act (proposed May 12, 2026):** Federal data broker framework bill; early legislative stage. No new movement found today.
