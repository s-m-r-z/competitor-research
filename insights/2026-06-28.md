## Regulatory / Industry Developments
- **California DROP — 33 days to enforcement**: August 1, 2026 remains the hard enforcement deadline. The DROP platform launched January 1, 2026 and allows California residents to submit a single deletion request to all 575+ registered brokers for free. Data brokers must check DROP every 45 days and delete matching records within 90 days. $200/request/day penalty structure is driving significant compliance activity.
- **Senate investigation findings**: 2026 investigation found major brokers (Comscore, IQVIA Digital, Telesign, 6sense Insights) placed robots.txt-style blocking code on their opt-out pages to prevent search engine indexing — directly strengthening the case for paid services that handle these friction points programmatically.
- **Privacy legislation tailwind**: Industry identity theft losses from data broker breaches estimated at $21 billion annually (2026 congressional findings). Growing bipartisan attention may push federal data broker legislation forward before year-end.
