## Emerging / Regulatory
- **California DROP approaching enforcement**: Starting August 1, 2026 (27 days away), data brokers must access the platform every 45 days and delete data within 90 days. The DROP now covers 600+ registered brokers. This is the single biggest near-term market variable — a free state-backed opt-out tool that could reduce conversion rates for paid services among California residents
- **Incogni vs. Cloaked** comparison content appeared in search results, suggesting Cloaked is gaining enough traction to attract direct comparison articles — worth monitoring as a potential emerging competitor
